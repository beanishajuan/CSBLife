Guide Questions:
1. What can you do to prevent what happened in the 2nd part of the story? 
We should love things around us and not destroy it. We should take care of our environment just as it has taken care of us. 
2. How does it affect you as part of the Lasallian community? 
It helps us to be aware of our surroundings. It helps us know how we should act accordingly. 
3. Give at least 2 Benildean Core Values shown on the production 
Being socially competent, appreciative of the uniqueness of each indivual

4. How can you share these values to others?
We should excel in nourish the core values that we have so that others will be inspired by us. We should also teach others our talents.
5. Give your own personal insights about the play.
The play shows to us the tragedy that man has caused to our planet. It teaches us that we should be responsible for the things that we do, where there is a consequence for every action that we do.
